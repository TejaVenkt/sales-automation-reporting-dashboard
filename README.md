# Sales Intelligence & Invoice Tracking Dashboard

## Overview

Designed and developed an Excel-based reporting and tracking solution to manage vehicle sales data across multiple financial years. The tool enables users to search invoices, track chassis-level information, monitor dealer performance, and analyze sales distribution across business zones through an interactive dashboard.

The project consolidates 17k+ records spanning multiple financial years and provides a centralized reporting interface for business and operations teams.


## Business Problem

Sales data was distributed across multiple files and financial years, making invoice tracking, chassis verification, and management reporting time-consuming.

The business required:

- Centralized sales visibility
- Quick invoice lookup
- Chassis-level traceability
- Zone-wise performance reporting
- Dealer-level tracking
- Faster management reporting

  ## Solution

Developed an Excel-based reporting platform using:

- Power Query
- Pivot Tables
- XLOOKUP
- Data Validation
- Dynamic Search Functions
- Interactive Dashboard Components

The solution consolidates sales information from multiple financial years into a single reporting environment.
